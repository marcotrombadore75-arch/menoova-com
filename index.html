Questions answered.

Is it really free until December? +

Yes, completely. No credit card, no trial, no catch. You get a fully functional digital menu at zero cost until December 2026.

What happens in December? +

We reach out and ask if you want to continue. If yes, you lock in your founding member rate forever. If no, your menu goes offline — no charges, no questions asked.

What does "locked forever" mean? +

As long as you stay subscribed, your price never increases. New customers after January 2027 will pay standard rates — you won't.

How do I get my menu ready? +

Just email us at hello@menoova.com with your menu content — a photo, a PDF or a list of items is fine. We take care of everything else in 48 hours.

Do my customers need to download an app? +

No. They simply scan the QR code with their phone camera and the menu opens instantly in the browser. No app, no registration.

Can I update my menu later? +

Yes. Just email us the changes and we'll update your menu within 24 hours. Updates are always included in your plan.
